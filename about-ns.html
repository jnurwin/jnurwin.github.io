The Start

Born in a small town south of Louisville, Isaac J. Fox
found meaning in the pursuit of clothing,
accesories and above all else fashion. Learning
from his grandmother he started with simple
pieces of cloth before slowly moving on to a
much grander goal. A real piece of clothing.

A Coat

With the skills to put cloth and thread together
Isaac now made his greatest piece. To the right
you can see his vision. A real coat made by his
own hands. But he had more in mind than just
a warm and comfy coat. He wanted to create
something for others to enjoy and love.

Fashion Future

Isaac began to hone his skills and carve out a
vision for his craft. Now he brings to the world a
fresh and unique style. Every piece is made by
hand with the thought and passion to bring it
to life. When you wear Nova Solis fashion you can
be certain that you are wearing a bit of the future.
